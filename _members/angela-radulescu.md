Angela is an Assistant Professor in the Departments of Psychiatry and Neuroscience at the Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai, and core faculty in the Mount Sinai Center of Computational Psychiatry. They are interested in how people learn from interaction with their environment, and when and how learning can lead to changes in mental health. Angela enjoys photography, hiking, travel and trying a new sport every few years.
