## March 2026: Congratulations on your defense, Jackie!

Jackie Beltrán successfully defended her dissertation, *Capturing Neural and Digital Signatures of Depression: From 7-Tesla Neuroimaging to Real-World Behavior*. Congratulations, Dr. Beltrán! Check out her work in [this preprint](https://www.researchsquare.com/article/rs-7166201/v1).

## February 2026: Congratulations on your defense, Christina!

Christina Maher, the lab's first graduate student, successfully defended her dissertation, *The Neuronal Basis of State Representation in the Human Prefrontal Cortex*. Congratulations, Dr. Maher! Check out her work in these preprints: [Coordinated human prefrontal dynamics sustain task-state representations during learning](https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.64898/2026.05.03.722562v2) and [Learning to Attend Through Value-Based Hypothesis Testing](https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.64898/2026.03.11.710688v2).
