# {% include icon.html icon="fa-solid fa-handshake" %}Get Involved

## Location

We are part of the [Mount Sinai Center for Computational Psychiatry](https://icahn.mssm.edu/research/center-for-computational-psychiatry), an interdisciplinary research center studying how quantitative tools and methodologies can be used to improve mental health diagnosis and treatment.

## Prospective graduate students

If you are a prospective graduate student looking to join the lab, Mount Sinai offers several relevant programs: a [PhD in Neuroscience](https://icahn.mssm.edu/education/phd/neuroscience), a [PhD in Biomedical Sciences with a concentration in AI and Emerging Technologies in Medicine](https://icahn.mssm.edu/education/phd/biomedical-sciences/artificial-intelligence), and an [MD/PhD](https://icahn.mssm.edu/education/admissions/apply). You are welcome to send a letter of intent to Dr. Radulescu, though that is not required. We will consider every application.

## Postdoctoral fellows

If you are interested in joining the lab as a postdoctoral fellow, please send a letter of intent and your CV to Dr. Radulescu ([angela.radulescu@mssm.edu](mailto:angela.radulescu@mssm.edu)).
